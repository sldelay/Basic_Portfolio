About Me

Hello, thank you for taking the time to learn about me! In 2012, I started my journey in the coffee industry. I have spent the past seven years becoming an expert in all things coffee. I am now a part-owner at a coffee shop called Union Coffee Company, based in Milford, New Hampshire.


I currently live in Milford, New Hampshire with my wife and two daughters. In my free time, which is limited, I really enjoy woodworking and exploring new places.


I have had a lot of fun spending all of my career so far in the coffee world, but I have decided to make a career move. In December of 2019, I started a full stack web development bootcamp with UNH in Portsmouth, New Hampshire. My goal is to lay down a strong foundation in my knowledge and skillset as a developer and hopefully find a job in the field!
